About me

I am a blogger, a mentor and a data scientist who now in the industry for more than 7 years. Since the beginning, I have been passionate about data and its impact on the conventional structural and operational chain of businesses. So when I got to explore this field further, I was sure to utilize it to its full potential. Therefore I completed my graduation and then headed towards pursuing my PGDM from the International Institute of Information Technology Banglore. But my quest for going deeper into the field wasn't satisfied yet, so I pursued a Master of Science in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence from Liverpool John Moores University. Then, began my professional journey, where I worked on different projects and focused on implementing my learnings into practicality. If you look at my portfolio, you will see how I have managed to keep a substantial approach while diversifying my fields of application. Therefore, I am all up for helping organizations through my experience and skills while always learning something new.
